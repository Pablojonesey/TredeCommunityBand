Tredegar Community Brass Band

Background and Invitation to new players

The Tredegar Community Band are comprised of players at all levels of experience, ranging from new to experienced players who have played for many years. We rehearse each Tuesday from 7:30 pm to 9:00 pm at the Alwyn Powell (Tredegar Town) Band Hall, address below. We have some spare cornets, tenor horn and brass instruments that you can use and it won't cost you a penny!.

We would be pleased to welcome new players to the band, whatever your level of experience or ability. Even if you have not played a brass instrument previously, we can coach you to a level sufficient to play in the band. If you are interested or want to learn more, give Julie Rooke a ring on 00000000000 or send her a text message with your contact details.Rehearsals take place each Tuesday at:
